Our Story

Every ministry begins with a calling.

Our Beginning

Divine Alignment Ministries™ was born during a season when God met us in the midst of deep disappointment, heartbreak, and church hurt. At a time when trust had been broken and questions seemed louder than answers, God began revealing His heart in a way that separated who He is from the actions of those who had misrepresented Him.

Through that journey, He revealed that many of His people were quietly carrying wounds inflicted by those entrusted to shepherd, encourage, and care for them. Some had experienced rejection. Others had endured manipulation, spiritual abuse, condemnation, or neglect. For many, the pain had become so deep that they began questioning not only the church, but their relationship with God Himself.

From that place of healing and revelation, Divine Alignment Ministries™ was established to help people rediscover the heart of God through His Word, experience authentic community, and grow in a life-giving relationship with Jesus Christ. We believe the failures of people should never define the character of God, and that His love, truth, and faithfulness remain constant even when people fall short.

Our desire is to create spaces where biblical truth is shared with grace, conversations are authentic, worship is genuine, prayer is powerful, and every person is reminded that God still calls, restores, heals, and leads His people into divine alignment with His purpose.

The Foundation of Our Faith

At Divine Alignment Ministries™, our beliefs are more than a statement of doctrine—they are the foundation upon which every conversation, every prayer, every act of worship, and every ministry opportunity is built. While our journey began during a season of healing, our ministry is firmly established upon the unchanging truth of God's Word and the finished work of Jesus Christ.

We recognize that many people have experienced disappointment, confusion, or even hurt in places where they expected to find hope. Because of this, we are committed to ensuring that every teaching, every resource, and every gathering reflects the heart of God as revealed through Scripture.

The Bible

God's revealed truth for faith and life.

What We Believe

The Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, revealing His truth, His character, and His plan for humanity. We believe Scripture is God's complete revelation for faith and daily living, providing wisdom, correction, encouragement, and guidance for every season of life.

Why It Matters

Every teaching, every ministry decision, every prayer, and every conversation at Divine Alignment Ministries™ begins with God's Word. Scripture—not culture, opinion, or tradition—is our final authority for faith and life.

Supporting Scriptures

  • John 17:17
  • 2 Timothy 3:16–17
  • Psalm 119:105
Jesus Christ

The foundation of our salvation and hope.

What We Believe

Jesus Christ is the Son of God, our Lord and Savior, whose life, death, burial, and resurrection provide the only way of salvation and reconciliation with God.

Why It Matters

Everything we do points people to Jesus Christ. Our mission is not centered on personalities or traditions, but on the transforming power of His Gospel and His invitation into relationship with God.

Supporting Scriptures

  • John 14:6
  • Romans 5:8
  • Acts 4:12
Salvation

God's free gift of grace through Jesus Christ.

What We Believe

Salvation is the free gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone and not by human effort or works.

Why It Matters

No one earns God's love or forgiveness. Salvation is available to every person who places their faith in Jesus Christ, offering hope, redemption, and eternal life.

Supporting Scriptures

  • Ephesians 2:8–9
  • Romans 10:9–10
  • John 3:16
The Holy Spirit

God's presence and power within every believer.

What We Believe

The Holy Spirit dwells within every believer, providing comfort, conviction, wisdom, guidance, spiritual gifts, and power to live according to God's purpose.

Why It Matters

We do not journey alone. The Holy Spirit teaches, strengthens, encourages, and empowers believers to faithfully follow Christ each day.

Supporting Scriptures

  • John 14:26
  • Acts 1:8
  • Galatians 5:22–23
Prayer

Communion with God that transforms lives.

What We Believe

Prayer is a vital expression of our relationship with God. We believe God hears, responds, strengthens, comforts, and works according to His perfect will. Prayer is not merely a religious practice—it is an ongoing conversation with our Heavenly Father.

Why It Matters

We believe lives are changed through prayer. It is where burdens are exchanged for peace, direction is received, faith is strengthened, and hearts are aligned with the will of God.

Supporting Scriptures

  • Philippians 4:6–7
  • James 5:16
  • Matthew 7:7–8
The Church

The body of Christ called to worship, love, and serve.

What We Believe

The Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, love one another, make disciples, serve with humility, and faithfully proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ throughout the world.

Why It Matters

The Church was never intended to be merely a building or an organization. It is a family of believers growing together, encouraging one another, and reflecting the love of Christ to a world in need of hope.

Supporting Scriptures

  • Matthew 28:19–20
  • Hebrews 10:24–25
  • Acts 2:42–47
Discipleship

Growing daily to become more like Christ.

What We Believe

Discipleship is a lifelong journey of spiritual growth, learning, and obedience as believers mature in God's Word and become more like Jesus Christ through the transforming work of the Holy Spirit.

Why It Matters

Faith was never meant to remain stagnant. We are continually being shaped, refined, and equipped so that our lives reflect Christ and impact those around us for His glory.

Supporting Scriptures

  • Luke 9:23
  • Colossians 2:6–7
  • 2 Peter 3:18
Restoration

God's redeeming work in every broken story.

What We Believe

We believe restoration remains at the heart of God's redemptive work. Through Jesus Christ, broken lives are made whole, hope is renewed, purpose is restored, and every person is invited into a transformed relationship with God.

Why It Matters

Divine Alignment Ministries™ was born from the reality that God still heals, restores, and redeems. No life is beyond His reach, no heart is beyond His healing, and no story is beyond His ability to rewrite for His glory.

Supporting Scriptures

  • Romans 8:28
  • 2 Corinthians 5:17
