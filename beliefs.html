What We Believe

The Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, revealing His truth, His character, and His plan for humanity. We believe Scripture is God's complete revelation for faith and daily living, providing wisdom, correction, encouragement, and guidance for every season of life.

Why It Matters

Every teaching, every ministry decision, every prayer, and every conversation at Divine Alignment Ministries™ begins with God's Word. Scripture—not culture, opinion, or tradition—is our final authority for faith and life.
