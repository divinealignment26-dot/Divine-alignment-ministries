Welcome

More Than a Church.
A Transformation Center.

Divine Alignment Ministries™ exists to help people encounter Jesus Christ, discover biblical truth, strengthen their faith, and live in Divine Alignment with God's purpose for their lives.

Through worship, biblical teaching, authentic conversations, prayer, discipleship, and Christ-centered media, we are committed to creating an environment where lives are healed, restored, equipped, and transformed by the power of God's Word.

Whether you are exploring your faith for the first time, returning to God after a difficult season, or seeking a deeper relationship with Christ, we invite you to grow, worship, connect, and experience the transforming love of Jesus with us.
