Welcome to Divine Alignment Ministries™

Divine Alignment Ministries™ exists to help people develop a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ through biblical teaching, heartfelt worship, authentic conversations, fervent prayer, and Christ-centered media that encourages spiritual growth and everyday discipleship.

Whether you are beginning your faith journey, searching for biblical truth, or looking for a community where you can grow, you'll find opportunities to learn, worship, connect, and be encouraged as you pursue God's purpose for your life.
